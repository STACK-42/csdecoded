# CSDecoded 

CSDecoded is a website designed in light of bridging the gap between computer science theory and real world application. most CS curricula teach abstract concepts without showing how they power the tools and how they are invloved in the the technologies we use every day. CSDecoded makes those connections more explicit, helping. you see the practical skills out of coures and industry relevance behind it.

## Problem Statement: The Abstraction Gap in CS Education

Theory of Computation often feels abstract and disconnected from practice, DFAs Transisation graphs, langauge, and accepted langauge, they make no sense regarding the real world, yet its core ideas are almost everywhere, regex engines, compilers and cybersecurity. The problem is not the theory itself, but the lack of a clear mental map from "what you learn" to "what you can do with it."

**Decoded solves the Curriculum-to-Practice Mapping problem:**

> "How does this theory translate into practical skills, tools, and technologies I’ll actually use?"
